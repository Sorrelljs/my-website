USABILITY



aka User Experience, is the art of making your website simple, user-friendly and easy to use. If you construct your website in a manner that’s too complicated to navigate you will lose business.

Over 1 billion people live with disabilities. If sites don't consider disabled users, they're missing out on a huge chunk of the market. I use alt-tags, descriptions, utizilise color control, descibe links and much more. Many sites skip these steps.

It takes extra time and thought and people often forget. I make sure that your site targets 100% of the world with internet access.
